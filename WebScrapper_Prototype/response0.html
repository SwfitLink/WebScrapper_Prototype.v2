Reviews (2)
  1. Great value - Johan, 4 June 2022

    Solid power supply at a great price, which is let down slightly by its cables. Only the 24-pin cable is wrapped. The PCIe cables are stiff, flat ribbon cables, which result in a messy graphics card connection. The CPU cables are also very stiff, flat ribbon cables, which due to the stiffness, is about 50 mm too short to make neat cable management possible. So, if cable management is important, I would recommend you consider something else. However, I got it into a Phanteks P400A and it works. Finally, my unit was supplied with a SA power cable in the sealed box, although the website states it is not supplied with such a cable.

  2. Little work horse - Philip, 13 September 2021

    I am really pleased with this power supply it has every thing you would need for a Good build and I Plan on using quite a few of them in the future. I am a little disappointed to see that the 750 only comes with 2 x PCIE cables that can support 4 x 8 pin connectors. The 850 is supplied with 3 of these, that for my purposes would have been great.
